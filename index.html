Garlic Parmesan Pasta!

A delicious buttery, garlic parmesan pasta the whole family will enjoy! Its a fast easy but mostly delicious meal!

Do you love comfort food thats flavorful and filling, that doesn't break the budget? Then this recipe is for you!

This recipe will have your family begging you to make it again and again, and its ready in under 30mins!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of your favorite pasta
  • 4tbs unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves minced garlic(or 1/2tsp garlic powder)
  • 1 cup of grated paremsan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste!
  • Fresh Parsley (to garnish)

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Once your water is boiling, add your pasta, and cook for 8 minutes, or until your desired doneness.
  3. Strain your pasta, but reserve about a half cup of your pasta water
  4. Heat a skillet, on medium-low heat, and melt your butter.
  5. Add your minced garlic, and heat until fragrant.
  6. Add your pasta to the butter and garlic, and toss the pasta until fully coated
  7. Once fully coated, add your grated fresh parmesan cheese, and mix until combined. If needed, use some of your reserved pasta water, to help with incorporating the pasta and garlic butter.
  8. Serve while fresh and warm, and garnish with parsley. You can add more cheese too!
ENJOY!
